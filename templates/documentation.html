Anime Filter

GET
https://hex-anime.vercel.app/anime?genre[]={slug}&status={slug}&page={number}

Filter anime berdasarkan genre, season, studio, status, tipe, dan urutan.

Parameters
ParameterTypeDescription
genre[]arrayFilter by genre (action, isekai, ...)
season[]arrayFilter by season (fall-2024, winter-2025)
studio[]arrayFilter by studio (mappa, 8bit, ...)
statusstringongoing · completed · upcoming · hiatus
typestringtv · ova · movie · special · ona · music
orderstringtitle · update · latest · popular · rating
pageintegerHalaman (default: 1)
